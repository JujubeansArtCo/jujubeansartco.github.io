Privacy Policy

Jujubeans Art Co | Last updated: 30 March 2026

1. Introduction

Jujubeans Art Co ("we", "us", or "our") operates as a digital art print shop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services or interact with our application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and email address (when you contact us or make a purchase)
  • Purchase and transaction data (processed securely via Etsy)
  • Usage data when you interact with our Pinterest integration

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Communicate with you about your purchases
  • Improve our products and services
  • Post artwork content to our own Pinterest account via the Pinterest API

4. Pinterest API Usage

Our application uses the Pinterest API solely to manage our own Pinterest business account. We use OAuth 2.0 authentication to create and schedule pins promoting our artwork. We do not collect, store, or share any Pinterest user data from third parties.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to fulfil your orders (e.g. Etsy, payment processors).

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a UK-based business, we comply with UK GDPR. You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at: dchesney@hotmail.com

8. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
