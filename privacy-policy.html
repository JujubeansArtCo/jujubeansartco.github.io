Privacy Policy

Jujubeans Art Co | Last updated: 30 March 2026

1. Introduction

Jujubeans Art Co operates as a digital art print shop. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect: name, email address, purchase data (processed via Etsy), and usage data from our Pinterest integration.

3. How We Use Your Information

To process orders, communicate about purchases, improve our services, and post artwork to our own Pinterest account via the Pinterest API.

4. Pinterest API Usage

Our application uses the Pinterest API solely to manage our own Pinterest business account via OAuth 2.0. We do not collect or share any third-party Pinterest user data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as required by law or to fulfil orders.
